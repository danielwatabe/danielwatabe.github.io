About Me

I am curious about everything. I decided to study computer science and statistics because their applications are endless. Working with data is like being at a specialty grocery store, figuring out what new dish I can make with an alien-looking vegetable. If you couldn’t tell from the food analogy, I love trying new foods and cooking with new ingredients. I’m always expanding my cultural and creative horizons and this is reflected in the way I approach my passions for food and technology.

I love learning about other people and cultures. I grew up in a multicultural household and realized that every culture has something unique to contribute. Because of my upbringing, I’m not afraid to mix and match things to make non-traditional creations. I’m not afraid to innovate.

I spend my free time vicariously living through food and travel videos or playing video games with my friends. I think technology is just like food: it brings us all together regardless of how bitter or sweet it can be.

Hot Bagels-N-More

Created the first site for this bagel shop around their pre-existing logo. After understanding the owner's desires, I decided on a one page site. I didn't want users to have to go through multiple pages to find the menu or hours. I also linked up all the necessary social media accounts as well as tidying them up.
This place has some of the best bagels.

  • Date: June - July 2016
  • Website: Hot Bagels-N-More
  • Tools: Bootstrap, CSS, and HTML
